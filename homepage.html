MC Learning and Associates is a group of professional educators that provide an at-home, small group learning environment for children ages 4 - 11, serving up to 12 children.


Our philosophy is to create the world we aspire for ourselves, our children, and our community. We will provide an educational environment that promotes universal love, truth, and respect for all life. We will strive to build confidence and encourage creativity, self-awareness, and joy. Our teaching pedagogy focuses on interest, project-based themes, core academics, and hands-on outside play.


All children will be academically aligned with the expectation of the Nevada State School Systems.
